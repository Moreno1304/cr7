History

Gunner

CR7 scored 691 goals with the teams he played for throughout his career (Sporting, Manchester United on two occasions, Real Madrid and Juventus), and 115 with Portugal, which also makes him the top scorer in the history of the national teams.

Before the fame?

He became one of the most expensive players in football history after transferring from Manchester United to Real Madrid, in a deal worth approximately 94 million euros. He started his career in a team from the city where he was born, but in 1995 he went to Clube Desportivo Nacional.
